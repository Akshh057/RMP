About Us

Rajasthan Minerals Production (RMP) is a premier mineral extraction and processing company based in the mineral-rich state of Rajasthan, India. With decades of experience in the mining industry, we specialize in the extraction, processing, and distribution of high-quality minerals. Our commitment to sustainable mining practices and environmental responsibility sets us apart in the industry.
